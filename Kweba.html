WATERFALLS IN SIBUYAN

Name Description Picture
KWEBA FALLS Kweba Falls is a unique waterfall located inside an open cave in Sibuyan Island. Reaching it requires a challenging trek through dense forests, so hiring a local guide is recommended. Upon arrival, visitors will find a lagoon inside the cave where water cascades down, offering a one-of-a-kind experience. The place feels like a hidden paradise in the middle of the jungle, ideal for adventurers and nature lovers looking to discover secret natural wonders.
