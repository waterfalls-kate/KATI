BILA-BILA FALLS Bila-Bila Falls was Located in Barangay Canjalon, San Fernando, Bila-Bila Falls is a stunning waterfall surrounded by the lush forests. Its name comes from the local term "bila-bila", which means "butterfly", due to the abundance of butterflies in the area. Water cascades down into a crystal-clear natural poil, perfect for swimming and relaxation. The falls are easily accessible via a short walk from the main road, making it a popular destination for locals and tourists alike.
