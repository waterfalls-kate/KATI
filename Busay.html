WATERFALLS IN SIBUYAN

Name Description Picture
BUSAY FALLS Busay Falls is a well-known destination for locals, located in Barangay Panangcalan, San Fernando. The waterfall has clear water that cascades into a natural pool, ideal for swimming. The surroundings are filled with large rocks and lush vegetation, providing natural shade and a cool breeze. The journey to the falls is relatively easy, requiring only a short walk from the drop-off point, making it a popular choice for families and friends looking to swim and have a picnic.
