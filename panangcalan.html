PANANGCALAN FALLS Panangcalan Falls is located in San Fernando, Sibuyan Island, Romblon Province, Philippines. Panangcalan Falls is situated in the interior of San Fernando, approximately 10-15 minutes away from the town center. The falls can be reached by trekking through a scenic trail that winds through lush forests and along rivers.

The falls is approximately 20 meters (66 feet) high, with a single cascade that drops into a natural pool.

The natural pool at the base of the falls is perfect for swimming and relaxing. The crystal-clear waters are refreshing and invigorating.

The trek to Panangcalan Falls offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape, including rice fields, forests, and rivers. Along the way, visitors can spot various wildlife, such as birds, monkeys, and reptiles.
