CANTINGAS RIVER Cantingas River, situated in Barangay Taclobo, San Fernando, is known as one of the cleanest rivers in the Philippines. The water originates from the mountains of Mt. Guiting-Guiting, flowing clear and cold. Some sections of the river are deep enough for swimming, and there are platforms for cliff diving, making it an exciting experience for adventure seekers. The river is surrounded by lush greenery, providing fresh air and scenic views. It is a perfect spot for families and friends looking to picnic and enjoy nature.
