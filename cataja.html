CATAJA FALLS Cataja Falls is one of the most stunning waterfalls in Sibuyan Island, Philippines. Here are some fascinating facts about this natural wonder: Cataja Falls is situated in the municipality of Magdiwang, Sibuyan Island, Romblon Province. The falls can be reached by hiking through a scenic trail that winds through lush forests and along rivers. Cataja Falls is approximately 30 meters (100 feet) high, with a series of cascading tiers that create a breathtaking spectacle. The falls are surrounded by a natural amphitheater of limestone cliffs and lush vegetation.

Swimming and Relaxation; The base of the falls features a natural swimming pool, perfect for cooling off and relaxing. The crystal-clear waters are refreshing and invigorating, making it an ideal spot to unwind.
