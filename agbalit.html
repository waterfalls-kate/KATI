AGBALIT RIVER Agbalit River is situated in the municipality of Magdiwang, Sibuyan Island, and flows through a rugged terrain of mountains, valleys, and forests.

The river is known for its crystal-clear waters, which cascade over rocks and boulders, creating a series of rapids and waterfalls. The surrounding landscape is lush and verdant, with tropical vegetation and towering trees.

Swimming and Relaxatin; Agbalit River offers several swimming areas, including natural pools and waterfalls. The river's crystal-clear waters are perfect for swimming, relaxing, and rejuvenating.

Visitors can trek along the river's banks, exploring the surrounding forests and discovering hidden waterfalls and swimming areas. The trek can be challenging, but the scenic views and opportunities to spot wildlife make it a rewarding experience.
